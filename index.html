For this assignment, we were tasked with engaging in auto-ethnographic research of a specific digital interaction of our choosing. With the information that we gathered, we were instructed to create an infographic. For my digital interaction, I chose the trending online game 'Wordle'. At the time of the project, I was playing the game every day and thought it would be a good interaction to track. For my infographic, I was first deciding between creating an animation or a poster, eventually deciding to create an animation. I struggled to convey my information interestingly at first but through the critiques, I think I was able to properly and creatively convey the information that I wanted to.

Inspiration

A lot of my inspiration for the infographic came from the game 'Wordle'.

Process

The first couple of weeks was focused on research. The final weeks, the critiques helped me focus my ideas and properly convey the information I gathered.
